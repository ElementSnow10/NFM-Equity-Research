# Alert Rules (Locked v1 Core Metrics - Complete List)

ALERT_1:
Parameter: ROE
Condition: < 15%
Severity: High
Meaning: Return on Equity below shareholder expectations; inefficient use of equity base.

ALERT_2:
Parameter: ROCE
Condition: < 15%
Severity: High
Meaning: Core capital efficiency below premium threshold indicates poor long-term compounding potential.

ALERT_3:
Parameter: Net Profit Margin
Condition: < 8%
Severity: Medium
Meaning: Thin bottom-line margins leave little room for error or cost increases.

ALERT_4:
Parameter: Operating Margin
Condition: Decline > 150 bps YoY
Severity: Medium
Meaning: Deteriorating core business profitability and pricing power.

ALERT_5:
Parameter: Revenue CAGR (3yr)
Condition: < 8%
Severity: Medium
Meaning: Top-line growth lagging behind market inflation and competitor benchmarks.

ALERT_6:
Parameter: Profit CAGR (3yr)
Condition: < Revenue CAGR (3yr)
Severity: High
Meaning: Margin compression; company is growing sales but becoming less profitable.

ALERT_7:
Parameter: Debt-to-Equity
Condition: > 1.5
Severity: High
Meaning: Elevated leverage ratio raising concerns about financial solvency during downturns.

ALERT_8:
Parameter: Interest Coverage
Condition: < 3.0
Severity: High
Meaning: Operating profits barely covering interest expenses; high default risk.

ALERT_9:
Parameter: Operating CF Ratio
Condition: < 1.0
Severity: Critical
Meaning: Operating cash flow insufficient to cover short-term liabilities (Liquidity risk).

ALERT_10:
Parameter: Free Cash Flow
Condition: Negative for current year
Severity: Critical
Meaning: Business consuming cash rather than generating it; unsustainable without external funding.

ALERT_11:
Parameter: FCF Margin
Condition: < 5%
Severity: High
Meaning: Poor conversion of revenue into distributable cash.

ALERT_12:
Parameter: Asset Turnover
Condition: < 0.7
Severity: Medium
Meaning: Inefficient utilization of asset base to generate sales.

ALERT_13:
Parameter: Earnings Volatility
Condition: > 20% Coefficient of Variation
Severity: Medium
Meaning: Unstable earnings stream reducing predictability and "quality" of stock.
