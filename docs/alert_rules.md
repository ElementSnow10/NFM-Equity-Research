# Alert Rules

ALERT_1:
Parameter: ROCE
Condition: Drop > 20% YoY
Severity: High
Meaning: Core business efficiency deterioration

ALERT_2:
Parameter: Revenue Growth
Condition: Growth < 5% YoY for 2 consecutive quarters
Severity: Medium
Meaning: Potential stagnation in market share or demand

ALERT_3:
Parameter: Net Profit Margin
Condition: Margin < 10%
Severity: High
Meaning: Profitability issues potentially due to rising costs

ALERT_4:
Parameter: Debt-to-Equity Ratio
Condition: Ratio > 2.0
Severity: High
Meaning: High leverage indicating increased financial risk

ALERT_5:
Parameter: Operating Cash Flow
Condition: Negative for current fiscal year
Severity: Critical
Meaning: Inability to generate cash from core operations

ALERT_6:
Parameter: Current Ratio
Condition: Ratio < 1.0
Severity: High
Meaning: Liquidity crunch; risk of not meeting short-term obligations

ALERT_7:
Parameter: Inventory Turnover
Condition: Drop > 15% YoY
Severity: Medium
Meaning: Efficiency loss in inventory management or slowing sales
